Decorative & Faux Finishes

  • Kitchen & bathroom cabinets
  • Built-ins, shelving & mantels
  • Staining, sealing & restoration

Surface Prep & Repair

The secret to a flawless paint job is flawless prep. We patch drywall, repair plaster, fill nail holes, sand rough spots, caulk gaps, and prime every surface before a drop of paint goes on.

  • Drywall & plaster repair
  • Wallpaper removal
  • Sanding, caulking & priming
