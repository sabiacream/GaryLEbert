What We Do

Our Services

Whether you own a home or business, we offer complete solutions from simple paint touch-ups to full-scale remodeling and restoration. No job too big or too small.

Interior & Exterior Painting

Professional painting services for residential homes and commercial properties. We use premium paints and expert techniques for a flawless, lasting finish.

  • Custom color consultation
  • Trim, doors & ceilings
  • Power washing & prep

Custom Decorative / Faux Painting

Specializing in unique decorative painting techniques including faux finishes, venetian plaster, and custom murals that transform any space.

  • Faux & specialty finishes
  • Wallpaper removal
  • Texture application

Drywall & Plaster Repair

Expert drywall installation, patching, and repair work. We restore walls to a smooth, perfect finish ready for painting or wallpaper.

  • Hole & crack patching
  • Full drywall installation
  • Smooth finish texturing

Remodeling & Restoration

Complete remodeling services for bathrooms, kitchens, basements, and garages. We bring your vision to life with quality materials and craftsmanship.

  • Kitchen & bathroom remodels
  • Basement finishing
  • Garage conversion & upgrades

Carpentry & Shelving

Custom-built closet systems, shelving, cabinetry, and finish carpentry that maximizes your space and matches your style perfectly.

  • Custom closet systems
  • Built-in shelving
  • Trim & molding work

Home Maintenance

Comprehensive home maintenance programs for residential and commercial clients. We keep your property in top condition year-round.

  • Preventative maintenance
  • Minor repairs & fixes
  • Commercial property care
