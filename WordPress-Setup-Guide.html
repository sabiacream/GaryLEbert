Step 2 ‚Äî Install WordPress (5 Minutes)

All major hosting providers include a 1-click WordPress installer. Here's how it works with Bluehost (others are very similar):

  • 1
    Log in to your hosting control panel (cPanel or hPanel) After signing up for hosting, you'll receive login details via email.
  • 2
    Find "WordPress" or "Install WordPress" Look for the WordPress icon in the "Website" or "Website Builders" section.
  • 3
    Click "Install" and fill in your site details Site name: "Gary L. Ebert & Co." ‚Äî Username: your choice ‚Äî Password: something strong.
  • 4
    Wait 2‚Äì3 minutes for installation to complete You'll receive a link to your WordPress dashboard (usually yoursite.com/wp-admin).
  • 5
    Log in at yoursite.com/wp-admin You're now inside your WordPress dashboard! The hard part is done.

Step 4 ‚Äî Install Essential Plugins

Beautiful photo galleries for your portfolio section. Drag-and-drop gallery management. Free version available.

Backup

Automatic backups of your site to Google Drive or Dropbox. Essential ‚Äî always have a backup!

Social / Reviews

Display your Google Reviews on the website automatically. Great for building trust with new visitors.

    Embed on your Contact page Save the form, then on your Contact page in Elementor, add a "WPForms" widget and select your form.

Step 9 ‚Äî SEO Setup & Going Live

Yoast SEO Setup:

  • Go to SEO ‚Üí General and run the setup wizard
  • Site title: "Gary L. Ebert & Co. | Painter & Home Improvement Lehigh Valley PA"
  • On each page, fill in the "Yoast SEO" section at the bottom with a title and meta description

Google Business Profile:

  • Go to business.google.com and claim/create your listing
  • This is FREE and helps you appear in local Google searches for "painter near me"
  • Add your photos, hours, phone number, and website

SSL Certificate:

  • Most hosts include a free SSL (the padlock icon in the browser). Enable it in your hosting panel
  • In WordPress: Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí Change "http://" to "https://" in both URL fields
